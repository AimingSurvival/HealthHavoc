Your Guide to Healthy Weight Gain

Why Gaining Weight Matters

Being underweight carries risks like weakened immunity, low muscle mass, and higher mortality risk. Healthy weight gain isn’t just about looks — it improves energy, immunity, and quality of life.

Clean Bulking vs. Dirty Bulking

Comparison

  • Clean Bulking – Lean meats, whole grains, veggies, healthy fats, controlled calorie surplus.
  • Dirty Bulking – Junk food, sugar, fast food, rapid fat gain, nutrient deficiencies.

Macronutrient Distribution

  • 🍗 Protein: 1.6–2.2g/kg body weight
  • 🍚 Carbs: 40–65% of total calories
  • 🥑 Fats: 20–35% of total calories

Weight Gain Strategies

  • ✅ Add liquid calories (smoothies, shakes)
  • ✅ Add calorie-dense extras (cheese, nut butters, oils)
  • ✅ Focus on progressive resistance training (3–5x per week)
  • ✅ Ensure proper sleep (7–9 hours/day)
